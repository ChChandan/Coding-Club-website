About Us


Coding club, SRM- University AP is a student-run community exclusive to the students of SRM- University, AP. The aim of this club is to learn the art of coding and increase the joy of programming. The goal of this club is to cherish the appetite for learning modern technologies and shape them through programming. We are a bunch of welcoming, friendly coding enthusiasts. Here we try to take the fear of programming from the members away. We try to inspire and motivate members to excel in life with the help of coding.

The club also aims to provide a platform to nourish the technical skills of its members through various activities. During these activities, the members can learn about new technologies, showcase their skills and explain concepts to the rest of the members so that everyone can learn and grow together.


Our Vision


To facilitate members with the knowledge, confidence, and opportunity to make a difference in the world.

The club's major goal is to strengthen the programming skills of its members. The club will encourage them for prestigious contests such as ACM-ICPC, Google Code Jam, and different national and international hackathons. The coding club brings the coding expertise together under one roof. Coding club activities consist of multiple thrust areas like Big data analytics, Internet of Things, Ethical hacking, Web Development, Android Development, Quantum Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine learning.
